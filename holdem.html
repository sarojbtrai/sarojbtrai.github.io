Introduction

Make the best 5-card hand to win from 2 private cards and 5 community cards.

Game Flow
  1. Each player is dealt 2 hole cards face down.
  2. Pre-flop betting round (starting left of big blind).
  3. Flop: 3 community cards dealt face up.
  4. Second betting round.
  5. Turn: 4th community card dealt.
  6. Third betting round.
  7. River: 5th and final community card.
  8. Final betting round.
  9. Showdown: Remaining players reveal hands; best 5-card hand wins.
